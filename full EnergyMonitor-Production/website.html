Real-Time Voltage Monitoring

Phase-to-phase and phase-to-neutral voltage across all 3 phases, updated every second. Instant alerts on sag, swell, or imbalance beyond your configured thresholds.

  • R, Y, B phase individual tracking
  • Phase imbalance % calculation
  • Voltage sag/swell event logging
  • Historical min/max trend charts
  • TNEB supply quality compliance report
🔄

Current Monitoring

Non-invasive clip-on CT sensors measure per-phase current with ±0.5% accuracy. Track motor load, detect overcurrent, and catch unbalanced loading before it damages equipment.

  • Per-phase current (A RMS)
  • Neutral current monitoring
  • Overcurrent alarm with delay timer
  • Load profile 24h / 7d / 30d
  • Motor health trend analysis
💡

Power Monitoring

Active (kW), reactive (kVAR), and apparent (kVA) power for each phase and total system. Power factor correction suggestions included in weekly reports.

  • Active / Reactive / Apparent power
  • Power factor per phase and total
  • Peak demand tracking (kVA)
  • Harmonic distortion indicator
  • Penalty-free PF recommendations
📈

Energy Consumption

Cumulative kWh tracking per machine, per shift, per day, week, and month. Compare energy intensity across production lines to find and fix energy waste.

  • Per-machine kWh metering
  • Shift-wise energy breakdown
  • Specific Energy Consumption (SEC)
  • kWh per kg yarn / kWh per piece
  • ISO 50001 compatible reporting
💰

Electricity Bill Monitoring

Live bill projection based on TNEB HT/LT/ToD tariff rates. Know your electricity cost at any moment of the billing month — no surprises when the bill arrives.

  • TNEB LT/HT tariff rate engine

  • Peak demand charge projection
  • Month-to-date bill accumulation
  • Budget alert at configurable % threshold

Common Questions
Pricing FAQs
Is the hardware cost included in the monthly fee? ▾
Hardware (the physical PG-3M logger and CTs) is a one-time cost separate from the monthly software subscription. For Enterprise customers with 10+ machines, hardware is included. We also offer a full rental model where you pay a slightly higher monthly fee and we own the hardware.
Can I try before committing? ▾
Yes. We offer a 30-day free pilot on one machine at your site. Our engineer installs the hardware at no cost, and you get full Professional plan access for 30 days. If you're not satisfied, we remove the hardware at no charge.
How is "per machine" defined? ▾
One machine = one 3-phase power circuit monitored by one PG-3M logger. For a ring frame with a single 3-phase supply, that is one machine. If you have a MCC panel feeding multiple circuits you want tracked separately, each circuit counts as one machine.
Do you support GST-compliant invoicing? ▾
Yes. All invoices are GST-compliant (18% GST on software services). Hardware purchases are taxed at 12% GST. We provide monthly auto-generated invoices via email for your accounts team.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▾
Your data is yours. On cancellation, we provide a complete data export in CSV and PDF format for the full retention period, at no charge. Data is deleted from our servers 90 days after export.
